## 📌 Project Overview
This interactive **Power BI Dashboard** provides deep analytical insights into the real estate market of Bengaluru, India. Utilizing the popular *Bengaluru House Price Dataset*, this project transforms messy, unstructured property listings into a clean, executive-level decision-making tool. 

It is specifically tailored for **construction companies, real estate investors, and urban developers** to understand pricing mechanisms, market benchmarks, and structural demand.

---

## 🛠️ Advanced Data Engineering & Visual Features
Using **Power Query** and advanced data modeling, the following technical solutions were implemented to maximize the dashboard's business value:

* **🔄 Dynamic Availability Grouping:** Applied conditional logic to bin messy text and raw date strings into two clean strategic operational groups: `Ready to Move` vs `Under Construction`, complete with dual-layer drill-down navigation.
* **⚠️ Outlier & Anomaly Identification:** Isolated and categorized structural anomalies (e.g., properties with an extreme number of bathrooms, up to 40) that could skew corporate pricing benchmarks.
* **🎛️ Interactive UI/UX Tile Slicers:** Implemented modern, web-style tile slicers for `Area Type` segmentation. This allows seamless cross-filtering across all global KPIs and visuals without creating visual noise or dead space.

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

### 1. 🏗️ The Construction Premium Paradox
Properties listed as **Under Construction** command a higher average market price compared to **Ready to Move** assets. This signals a strong developer premium rate and high customer demand for modern, upcoming housing complexes over older, ready-made listings.

### 2. 🚽 Structural Pricing Benchmarks
Average property valuation scales predictably alongside bathroom and balcony counts, peaking sharply at **8-bathroom premium configurations** before dropping down. This indicates that anything above 8 bathrooms enters a niche commercial or hostel/dormitory layout, which naturally has lower individual valuation benchmarks.

### 3. 📊 Market Overview at a Glance
* **Total Properties Tracked:** 13.32K listings
* **Average Market Price:** 113.38 Lakhs
* **Average Bathroom Count:** 2.69 per property
